Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a seller account?

Visit our Seller Login page and click on the registration link. You'll need to provide your business details and complete the verification process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money, Credit/Debit Cards via Paystack, and Bank Transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location. Most orders within Accra are delivered within 1-3 business days. Other regions may take 3-7 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Contact the seller directly through your order page to initiate a return.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your orders from your Orders Dashboard.

How can I become a verified seller?

After creating your seller account, submit your business documents through the verification portal. Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days.
