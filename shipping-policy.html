Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2024

ShopUp Ghana partners with reliable delivery services to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time across Ghana.

Quick Overview:
  • Free shipping on orders over GH₵ 200 within Accra
  • Delivery to all regions in Ghana
  • Most orders delivered within 2-7 business days
  • Track your order in real-time

1. Delivery Locations

We deliver to all regions in Ghana, including:

  • Greater Accra: Accra, Tema, Kasoa, Dodowa, etc.
  • Ashanti Region: Kumasi, Obuasi, Mampong, etc.
  • Western Region: Takoradi, Sekondi, Tarkwa, etc.
  • Central Region: Cape Coast, Winneba, Kasoa, etc.
  • Northern Regions: Tamale, Bolgatanga, Wa, etc.
  • All Other Regions: Contact us for confirmation

4. Order Processing

  • Order Confirmation: Instant email/SMS after successful payment
  • Seller Processing: 1-2 business days to prepare order
  • Dispatch: Notification when order ships
  • Tracking: Tracking number provided

5. Delivery Methods

We use trusted delivery partners:

  • Standard Courier: Professional delivery services
  • Express Courier: Priority next-day/same-day delivery
  • Pickup Stations: Collect from designated locations (major cities)
  • Store Pickup: Some sellers offer store pickup

6. Tracking Your Order

  1. Login to your ShopUp account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Click on order number
  4. View tracking information and delivery status
  5. Real-time updates via SMS/email

7. Delivery Requirements

You Must Provide:

  • Complete delivery address (house number, street, area)
  • Ghana Digital Address (if available)
  • Working phone number
  • Alternative contact (optional but recommended)
  • Delivery instructions (landmarks, gate codes, etc.)

On Delivery Day:

  • Be available to receive delivery
  • Valid ID may be required for high-value items
  • Inspect package before signing
  • Report damage immediately

8. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails:

  • 1st Attempt: Courier calls/SMS, reschedules
  • 2nd Attempt: Next business day
  • 3rd Attempt: Final attempt or return to sender
  • Unclaimed: Returned to seller after 7 days, refund minus shipping

9. Large/Heavy Items

For furniture, appliances, and bulky items:

  • Specialized delivery arrangements
  • Delivery to ground floor (stairs extra charge)
  • Schedule delivery appointment
  • Two-person delivery team
  • Additional fees may apply

10. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within Ghana. International shipping coming soon. Sign up for notifications.

11. Holidays & Special Periods

During holidays (Christmas, Easter, Independence Day):

  • Extended processing times (+1-2 days)
  • Limited express delivery availability
  • Order early for guaranteed holiday delivery

12. Damaged in Transit

If your package arrives damaged:

  1. Do not refuse delivery
  2. Accept and note damage on delivery document
  3. Take photos of package and damaged item
  4. Contact us within 24 hours
  5. We arrange replacement or refund
