1. Return Window

  • Physical Products: 7 days from delivery date
  • Defective Items: 14 days from delivery date
  • Wrong Items: 7 days from delivery date

To be eligible for return, items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags attached.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers)
  • Personal care items (cosmetics, underwear)
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Downloaded digital products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

3. Return Process

Step 1: Contact Seller

Reach out to the seller within the return window explaining the issue.

Step 2: Get Return Authorization

Seller will provide return instructions and authorization number.

Step 3: Ship Item Back

Package item securely and ship to seller's return address.

Step 4: Inspection & Refund

Seller inspects item. If approved, refund processed within 5-7 business days.

4. Refund Methods

  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Original Payment Method: 5-10 business days

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item was defective or wrong.

6. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact seller immediately with photo proof
  • Return shipping covered by seller
  • Correct item shipped at no extra cost

7. Cancellations

7.1 Before Shipment

  • Full refund if cancelled before seller ships
  • Contact seller or support immediately
  • Refund processed within 3-5 business days

7.2 After Shipment

  • Return policy applies
  • Refuse delivery and notify seller
  • Or accept and return per standard process

8. Disputes

If you and the seller cannot agree:

  • Contact ShopUp Support: support@shopup.com.gh
  • Provide order number and details
  • We'll mediate and reach a fair solution
  • Decision typically within 7-14 business days
