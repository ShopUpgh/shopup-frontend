Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2024
Last Updated: December 23, 2024
Company: ShopUp Ghana

Quick Summary: You have 7 days to return most products for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds processed within 5-10 business days. Certain items (perishables, hygiene products) cannot be returned.

1. Our Commitment to You

At ShopUp Ghana, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we offer a fair and straightforward return and refund process.

2. Return Window

You have 7 days from the date you receive your order to initiate a return.

  • Day Count Starts: From delivery date (not order date)
  • Request Deadline: Return must be requested within 7 days
  • After 7 Days: Returns are not accepted

3. Conditions for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet ALL of these conditions:

  • ✅ Unused: Item not used, worn, washed, or altered
  • ✅ Original Condition: Same condition as received
  • ✅ Original Packaging: In original box/packaging with all tags
  • ✅ Accessories: All accessories, manuals, and parts included
  • ✅ Receipt/Proof: Valid order number or receipt

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items CANNOT be returned for health, safety, or practical reasons:

  • ❌ Perishable Goods: Food, flowers, plants
  • ❌ Hygiene Products: Underwear, cosmetics, skincare (if opened)
  • ❌ Personal Care: Toothbrushes, razors, hair products (if opened)
  • ❌ Custom/Personalized Items: Made-to-order products
  • ❌ Digital Products: Software, downloads, e-books
  • ❌ Gift Cards: Non-refundable
  • ❌ Final Sale Items: Marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance - No Returns"

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged, defective, or wrong:

  • Act Quickly: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide Photos: Take clear photos showing the issue
  • Full Refund: We'll refund the full amount including shipping
  • Replacement Option: We can send a replacement if available
  • Return Shipping: We pay for return shipping of damaged items

6. How to Request a Return

Return Process (3 Easy Steps):

Step 1 - Request Return: Go to your Order History and click "Request Return"
Step 2 - Seller Approval: Wait for seller to approve (1-2 business days)
Step 3 - Ship & Refund: Ship item back, get refund once seller confirms receipt

6.1 Online Return Request

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Find the order and click "Request Return"
  4. Select reason for return
  5. Upload photos (if damaged)
  6. Submit request

6.2 Return Approval

The seller will review your request within 1-2 business days and either:

  • ✅ Approve: You'll receive return instructions and shipping address
  • ❌ Decline: You'll receive an explanation (e.g., past 7-day window)

6.3 Shipping the Return

Once approved:

  • Package Securely: Use original packaging if possible
  • Include Order Number: Write order number on package
  • Ship to Address: Use the address provided in approval email
  • Get Tracking: Keep your shipping receipt and tracking number
  • Who Pays:
    • Damaged/Defective: We pay shipping
    • Change of Mind: You pay shipping

7. Refund Process

7.1 Inspection

When seller receives your return, they will:

  • Inspect the item (1-2 business days)
  • Verify it meets return conditions
  • Approve or deny the refund

7.2 Refund Approval

If approved, you'll receive:

  • Full Refund: Product price + original shipping (if damaged)
  • Partial Refund: Product price only (if change of mind)
  • Restocking Fee: 10% may apply for opened electronics (if stated)

7.3 Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed to your original payment method:

  • Card Payments: 5-10 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-7 business days

Note: Timeline depends on your bank/mobile money provider.

7.4 Refund Notification

You'll receive email and SMS confirmation when:

  • Return is received by seller
  • Refund is approved
  • Refund is processed

8. Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges. If you want a different size, color, or product:

  1. Return the original item (follow return process above)
  2. Wait for refund
  3. Place a new order for the desired item

Tip: Order the new item before returning if you want it faster (subject to refund approval).

9. Cancellations

9.1 Before Shipping

You can cancel your order before it ships:

  • Within 1 Hour: Free cancellation, full refund
  • After 1 Hour (Before Shipping): May incur 5% processing fee
  • How to Cancel: Go to "My Orders" → Click "Cancel Order"

9.2 After Shipping

Once shipped, you cannot cancel. Options:

  • Refuse delivery (may incur return shipping fees)
  • Accept delivery and follow return process

10. Wrong or Missing Items

10.1 Wrong Item Delivered

If you received the wrong product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours
  • Provide order number and photo of item received
  • We'll arrange pickup at no cost to you
  • Correct item shipped immediately
  • Full refund if you prefer

10.2 Missing Items

If items are missing from your order:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide order number and missing item details
  • We'll investigate and either:
    • Ship missing item at no cost
    • Issue partial refund for missing items

11. Restocking Fees

Restocking fees may apply in these cases:

  • Opened Electronics: Up to 10% (if stated on product page)
  • Large/Bulky Items: Up to 15% (furniture, appliances)
  • Special Orders: Up to 20% (custom items)

No Restocking Fee: Damaged, defective, or wrong items.

12. Seller-Specific Policies

Some sellers may have additional return policies:

  • Check the product page for seller-specific terms
  • Seller policies must comply with our minimum 7-day standard
  • Contact seller directly for questions

13. International Orders

Currently, ShopUp Ghana only ships within Ghana. No international returns apply.

14. Refund Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Contact Support: Email support@shopup.gh with order details
  2. Escalate: Request manager review if unresolved
  3. Timeline: We'll respond within 2-3 business days
