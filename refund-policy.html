Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: December 13, 2025

Our Commitment: We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we'll work with you and the seller to make it right.

1. Return Eligibility

You may return items within 7 days of delivery if:

  • Item is defective or damaged
  • Wrong item was sent
  • Item doesn't match the description
  • Item is missing parts or accessories
  • Item arrived significantly different from listing photos

Items That Cannot Be Returned

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers)
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Intimate or sanitary goods (underwear, cosmetics once opened)
  • Digital products or downloads
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "No Returns"

2. Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • In original condition (unused, unworn, unwashed)
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number)
  • Reported within 7 days of delivery

3. How to Request a Return

Step 1: Contact the Seller

Within 7 days of delivery:

  1. Log into your ShopUp account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order and click "Request Return"
  4. Provide reason and upload photos if applicable

Step 2: Wait for Approval

The seller has 48 hours to review and approve or deny your return request. You'll receive an email notification with their decision.

Step 3: Return the Item

If approved:

  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and free gifts
  • Attach the return label (if provided)
  • Ship to the address provided by the seller

4. Return Shipping Costs

  • Defective/Wrong Item: Seller pays return shipping
  • Changed Mind: Buyer pays return shipping (if seller accepts voluntary returns)
  • Damaged in Transit: Seller pays return shipping

5. Refund Process

5.1 Inspection Period

Once the seller receives your return, they have 3 business days to inspect the item and approve the refund.

5.2 Refund Timeline

  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days
  • Card Payments: 5-10 business days (depends on your bank)
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Mobile Money refund to provided number

5.3 Refund Amount

Full refund includes:

  • Product price
  • Original shipping fee (if item was defective or wrong)
  • Taxes paid

Note: ShopUp platform fee (3%) is non-refundable for voluntary returns.

6. Exchanges

If you received the wrong size or color, you may request an exchange instead of a refund:

  • Contact the seller through "Request Return" and select "Exchange"
  • Specify what you'd like instead
  • Seller will confirm availability
  • Return original item and new item will be shipped

7. Cancellations

7.1 Before Shipping

You can cancel an order before it ships:

  • Go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order"
  • Full refund processed within 3-5 business days
  • No fees or penalties

7.2 After Shipping

Once an order has shipped, you cannot cancel it. You must wait for delivery and follow the return process if needed.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

8.1 Report Immediately

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  1. Take photos of the damage (packaging and product)
  2. Report within 48 hours of delivery
  3. Do not discard packaging
  4. Submit a return request with photos

8.2 Resolution Options

The seller will offer one of the following:

  • Full refund
  • Replacement item (if available)
  • Partial refund (if you keep damaged item)
  • Repair (if applicable)

9. Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item:

  • Report within 7 days
  • Provide photos of what you received
  • Seller will arrange return pickup or provide return label
  • Correct item shipped or full refund provided
  • No cost to you

10. Non-Delivery

If your order doesn't arrive within the estimated delivery time:

  1. Check tracking information in "My Orders"
  2. Wait 3 business days after estimated delivery date
  3. Contact the seller through the platform
  4. If unresolved, contact ShopUp support

If confirmed lost, you'll receive a full refund.

11. Seller Responsibilities

Sellers on ShopUp must:

  • Honor this refund policy
  • Respond to return requests within 48 hours
  • Process approved refunds within 3 business days
  • Accept returns for defective or misrepresented items

12. ShopUp Protection

If a seller refuses a valid return or doesn't process a refund:

  • Contact ShopUp support at support@shopup.gh
  • Provide order details and evidence
  • We'll mediate and may issue a refund from seller's account
  • Repeated violations may result in seller suspension

13. Payment Disputes

If you have an issue with a payment transaction:

  • Contact ShopUp support immediately
  • Do not initiate a chargeback before contacting us
  • We'll work with Paystack to investigate
  • Resolution typically within 5-7 business days

14. Special Circumstances

14.1 Holidays and Peak Periods

During holidays (Christmas, Easter, etc.), processing times may be extended by 2-3 business days.

14.2 Pre-Orders

Pre-order items can be cancelled anytime before shipping. Refunds processed immediately.

14.3 Bulk Orders

Orders of 10+ items may have different return policies. Check with seller before purchasing.
