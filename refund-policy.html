Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2024

At ShopUp Ghana, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our refund and return procedures.

Key Points:
  • 7-day return window for most items
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Refunds processed within 5-10 business days
  • Some items are non-returnable (see exclusions)

1. Return Window

You may return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided they meet our return conditions.

2. Eligible Items for Return

  • Electronics (unopened, with all accessories and documentation)
  • Clothing and fashion items (unworn, with tags attached)
  • Home appliances (unused, in original packaging)
  • Books and media (in resellable condition)
  • Furniture (unassembled, undamaged)

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene, safety, or practical reasons:

  • Opened personal care items (cosmetics, toiletries)
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants)
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Downloaded digital products
  • Gift cards and vouchers

4. Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet ALL of the following:

  • Time Frame: Returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Condition: Unused, unworn, and in original condition
  • Packaging: Original packaging, tags, labels intact
  • Accessories: All included accessories, manuals, and parts
  • Proof of Purchase: Order number or receipt

5. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Login: Go to your ShopUp account → Orders
  2. Select Order: Find the order and click "Request Return"
  3. Choose Reason: Select return reason and provide details
  4. Await Approval: Seller reviews request (24-48 hours)
  5. Ship Back: Once approved, ship item to seller's address
  6. Confirmation: Upload tracking number or delivery proof

6. Return Shipping Costs

  • Defective/Wrong Item: We cover return shipping costs
  • Change of Mind: Customer pays return shipping
  • Accra Metro Area: Free pickup may be available

7. Refund Processing

Timeline:

  • Seller inspection: 2-3 business days after receiving return
  • Refund approval: 1-2 business days
  • Refund to your account: 5-10 business days

Refund Method:

  • Original payment method (Mobile Money, Card, Bank)
  • ShopUp wallet credit (instant, can be used for future purchases)

Refund Amount:

  • Full product price
  • Original shipping fee (if item was defective/wrong)
  • Return shipping may be deducted (if customer's choice)

8. Exchanges

We accept exchanges for:

  • Different size (clothing, shoes)
  • Different color (if available)
  • Similar product of equal or greater value

Contact customer service to arrange an exchange instead of a return.

9. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Report Immediately: Within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide Evidence: Photos/videos of damage
  3. Keep Packaging: Do not discard original packaging
  4. Fast Track: Priority processing for defective items

10. Wrong Item Delivered

If you received the wrong item:

  • Contact us within 24 hours
  • We arrange immediate pickup
  • Correct item shipped at no extra cost
  • Full refund if correct item unavailable

11. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Items returned after 7 days (up to 14 days): 50% refund
  • Items with minor damage not reported at delivery
  • Missing accessories (if not seller's fault)
  • Items not in original packaging

12. Sale and Clearance Items

  • Sale items are returnable unless marked "Final Sale"
  • Clearance items marked "Non-Returnable" cannot be returned
  • Promotional bundles must be returned complete

13. Cancellations

Before Shipping:

  • Full refund if order not yet shipped
  • Cancel through your account or contact seller

After Shipping:

  • Cannot cancel once shipped
  • Use return process instead
  • Refuse delivery for immediate return processing
