Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2024
Last Updated: December 23, 2024
Company: ShopUp Ghana (The House of Aldens Holdings)

Quick Summary: We respect your privacy. We collect only necessary information to provide our service, protect it with industry-standard security, and never sell your data to third parties. You have full control over your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShopUp Ghana ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our e-commerce platform.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with:

  • Ghana Data Protection Act (Act 843, 2012)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • International best practices for data protection

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register or use our platform, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, phone number
  • Profile Information: Business name (for sellers), profile picture, bio
  • Delivery Information: Shipping address, city, region, Ghana digital address
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Paystack (we do NOT store card numbers)
  • Communication: Messages you send us, customer support inquiries

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use our platform, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, time spent on site
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device model
  • Location Data: IP address, approximate geographic location
  • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies (see Section 8)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We receive information from:

  • Paystack: Payment confirmation, transaction status
  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • Sentry: Error reporting (anonymous)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide Services: Process orders, manage your account, deliver products
  • Communication: Send order confirmations, shipping updates, customer support responses
  • Payment Processing: Complete transactions securely through Paystack
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, enhance features
  • Security: Prevent fraud, protect against unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meet tax requirements, respond to legal requests
  • Marketing: Send promotional emails (only with your consent, opt-out anytime)

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract: To fulfill our obligations to provide e-commerce services
  • Consent: When you agree to receive marketing communications
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our platform and prevent fraud
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Ghana tax laws and regulations

5. How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We share it only with:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Paystack: Payment processing (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)
  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Sentry: Error monitoring (anonymized data only)
  • Email Service: Order confirmation and notification emails

5.2 Sellers

When you place an order, we share your:

  • Name, phone number, delivery address with the seller (to fulfill your order)
  • Order details (products, quantities)

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by:

  • Ghana law enforcement or government agencies
  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Ghana Revenue Authority (for tax purposes)

6. Data Security

We protect your information with:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in encrypted databases (Supabase)
  • Access Control: Row Level Security (RLS) ensures users access only their own data
  • Password Security: Passwords hashed using industry-standard bcrypt
  • Payment Security: Card details handled exclusively by Paystack (PCI compliant)
  • Regular Monitoring: Automated error detection and security logging

No system is 100% secure. While we implement strong security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

7. Your Rights Under Ghana Data Protection Act

You have the following rights:

7.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Use our Privacy Settings page to export your data in JSON format.

7.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data through your account settings or by contacting us.

7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data. Note:

  • Order history will be retained for 7 years (Ghana tax law requirement)
  • Personal identifiers will be removed/anonymized
  • Request deletion through Privacy Settings

7.4 Right to Object

You can object to processing of your data for marketing purposes. Use the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email.

7.5 Right to Data Portability

You can receive your personal data in a machine-readable format (JSON) and transmit it to another service.

7.6 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Visit Privacy Settings (logged-in users)
  • Email: privacy@shopup.gh
  • Phone: +233 XX XXX XXXX

We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Keep you logged in, remember your cart
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how you use our platform (anonymous)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language, currency settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Account Data: Until you request deletion
  • Order History: 7 years (Ghana Revenue Authority requirement)
  • Payment Records: 7 years (tax compliance)
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics Data: 2 years

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at privacy@shopup.gh.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Supabase servers. While servers may be outside Ghana, we ensure:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Service providers comply with international data protection standards
  • Data transfers follow GDPR adequacy requirements

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in Ghana law or regulations
  • New features or services
  • Feedback from users or regulators

We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email to your registered address
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • In-app notification (if applicable)

Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
