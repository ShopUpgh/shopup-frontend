Welcome to ShopUp Ghana ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our e-commerce platform and related services.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register, use our services, or make purchases, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Business Information (Sellers): Business name, category, description, Ghana Card/TIN
  • Delivery Information: Physical address, digital address (GhanaPost GPS), landmarks
  • Payment Information: Mobile money numbers, payment method preferences (we do NOT store card details)
  • Communications: Messages, support tickets, reviews, and ratings

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, clicks, search queries
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address or GPS (with your permission)
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, order details, payment status

3.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations from Paystack
  • Social Media: Profile information if you sign in via social accounts
  • Verification Services: Identity verification data for sellers

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

4.1 Service Delivery

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Facilitate payments through mobile money and other methods
  • Arrange delivery to your specified location
  • Manage your account and provide customer support

4.2 Business Operations

  • Verify seller identities and prevent fraud
  • Process refunds and handle disputes
  • Maintain platform security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

4.3 Communications

  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Notify you of important platform changes

4.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with Ghana Data Protection Act and other laws
  • Respond to legal requests and prevent illegal activities
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Protect our rights and those of our users

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent for specific purposes
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to fulfill our contract with you
  • Legal Obligation: Required by Ghanaian law
  • Legitimate Interests: For fraud prevention, security, and service improvement

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We Share Data With:

  • Sellers: Order details necessary for fulfillment
  • Payment Processors: Paystack for transaction processing
  • Delivery Services: Contact and address information for delivery
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), email services, analytics
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to prevent crime

6.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Transfer data outside Ghana without proper safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to personal data
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with regular backups
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats
  • Vendor Security: All third-party providers meet security standards

8. Your Rights Under Ghana Data Protection Act

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

8.3 Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).

8.4 Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for marketing or research purposes.

8.5 Right to Data Portability

Request your data in a machine-readable format.

8.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time for processing based on consent.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Duration of account existence
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (as required by Ghana tax laws)
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn
  • Legal Claims: Duration of statute of limitations

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.
